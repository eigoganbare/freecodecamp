A Tribute Page of Eigo Ganbare

A team-teaching resource website for Japanese schools.

Model - an innovated way to teach English that includes active learning! For Japanese/non-Japanese Teachers of English and Assistant Language Teachers.

Visual Learning - through simple interactive worksheets. The touch and feel for students to be able to communicate with one another in English. Listening, reading, writing, and speaking. All in one!

Integration - of the traditional and modern teaching style. Blended and crafted with learning support extensions, such as the class pen pal project, the after school English club program, the international exchange event, and much more!

Future Prosper - of bringing equal English educational opportunities and resources to Japanese schools! And, broadening their understanding of other countries' diverse culture.

If you have time, learn about Eigo Ganbare and how it is contributing to the schools in Japan.
